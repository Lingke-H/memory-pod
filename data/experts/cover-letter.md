# Cover Letter Expert Playbook

Starter playbook from common, widely-published cover-letter best-practices.

- Open with a specific hook, not "I am writing to apply for...".
- Connect your experience directly to the role's stated needs.
- Show you researched the company; reference something specific about it.
- Tell one concrete story that proves a key skill with a result.
- Keep it under one page: 3-4 short, focused paragraphs.
- Match the company's tone; be warm but professional.
- Close with genuine enthusiasm and a clear call to action.
- Never restate the resume — add context the resume cannot.
