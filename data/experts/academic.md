# Academic Writing Expert Playbook

Starter playbook from common, widely-published academic-writing best-practices.

- State your thesis or central claim clearly and early.
- Support every claim with specific evidence and citations.
- Define key terms; remove ambiguity and vague qualifiers.
- Structure clearly: motivated intro, logical sections, firm conclusion.
- Use precise, formal tone; avoid hype and overclaiming.
- Acknowledge limitations and engage counterarguments honestly.
- Make each paragraph do one job; lead with its point.
- Prefer clarity over complexity; shorter sentences when possible.
