# Email & Business Writing Expert Playbook

Starter playbook from common, widely-published business-writing best-practices.

- State the purpose in the first sentence; lead with the ask.
- Use a clear, specific subject line that previews the content.
- Keep it short and skimmable; one topic per email.
- Put any action items and deadlines up top, not buried.
- Be polite and direct; cut hedging and filler words.
- Prefer short paragraphs and bullets over walls of text.
- End with a clear next step and who owns it.
- Match formality to the recipient and relationship.
