# Resume Expert Playbook

Starter playbook from common, widely-published resume best-practices. Replace
with a real coach's `.mpod` for authoritative guidance.

- Lead every bullet with a strong action verb (built, led, shipped, reduced).
- Quantify impact with concrete numbers: %, $, time saved, scale.
- Tailor the resume to the specific role and mirror its keywords.
- Put the most impressive and relevant items first.
- Show outcomes and results, not just duties or responsibilities.
- Cut filler, clichés, and phrases like "responsible for".
- Keep formatting clean and consistent; one page for early career.
- Make every line answer "so what?" — why it mattered.
