# Marketing Copy Expert Playbook

Starter playbook from common, widely-published marketing-copy best-practices.

- Lead with the customer's pain or desire, not the product.
- Make the benefit concrete and specific, not vague adjectives.
- Write to one person, in their own words and voice.
- Use plain, vivid language; cut jargon and hype.
- Back claims with social proof, specifics, and numbers.
- Keep one clear call to action per piece.
- Make the first line earn the second; hook fast.
- Edit ruthlessly: if a word does no work, cut it.
