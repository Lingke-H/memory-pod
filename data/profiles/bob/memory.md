# Bob Memory

Bob is a founder building B2B productivity software for small operations teams.

He prefers direct, persuasive writing that quickly explains the business value.
He likes concrete examples, crisp positioning, and language that can become a
sales email or landing page section.

Current projects:

- Designing an AI workflow assistant for customer success teams.
- Talking to early design partners in logistics and field services.
- Preparing investor updates that focus on traction, revenue, and retention.

Writing preferences:

- Start with the pain point.
- Make the ROI obvious.
- Keep the tone confident and practical.
- Avoid academic framing unless it helps credibility.
